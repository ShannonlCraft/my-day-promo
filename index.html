Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Called & Clear for?+
Called & Clear is for Christians who feel stuck, confused about their direction, or like they're doing everything right but still can't move forward. If you've been praying for clarity but the answers feel foggy, this was made for you.
Do I need any prior experience?+
No. You just need to be willing to engage honestly with the material. Whether you're new to faith or have been walking with God for decades, the Called & Clear process meets you where you are.
How much time do I need each week?+
Plan for 60 to 90 minutes for the weekly live call, plus optional time for your worksheets and reflection prompts between sessions (about 20 to 30 minutes).
Will calls be recorded?+
Yes. All live sessions will be recorded and made available to enrolled participants so you never miss a teaching even if life gets busy.
What makes this different from other purpose programs?+
Most programs start at "let's find your purpose." Called & Clear starts at "let's fix your identity first." You can't build lasting direction on a performance based foundation.
